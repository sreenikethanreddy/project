THE COUNTRY INDIA!!!

India, officially the Republic of India, is a country in South Asia. It is the second-most populous country in the world with over 1.3 billion people, and the seventh-largest country in the world by area. India is bordered by Pakistan to the west; Nepal, China, and Bhutan to the north; Bangladesh and Myanmar to the east; and the Indian Ocean to the south. The capital city is New Delhi.
